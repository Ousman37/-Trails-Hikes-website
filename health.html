Health & Safety Sustainability

Hiking in the woods of nature or top of the Mountain is a very sustainable way to vacation, and thus we make an extremely small footprint on nature. We mainly use the public transportation with our guests to and from hiking routes from outside the city . All garbage (our's or other's) is carried back and recycled accordingly. Our wish is that everyone should have good experience in beautiful world nature. Today and tomorrow.

Be humble

01 Get started

Be realistic

Scaling a 2,000-meter summit may seem possible from the perspective of your living room, but you need to make sure you are in good enough shape to do so! While it may be tempting to book the most demanding trail for your first hike, it may make sense to start with something a little easier. If you have a medical condition this also should be taken into account. Some trips may require vaccinations, be sure to check on the details provided.
